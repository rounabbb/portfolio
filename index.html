FAQ

Questions Indian business owners ask first

How are your prices this low?

I'm a solo developer, not an agency. No office rent, no sales team, no project managers taking a cut. Same quality as a ₹1 lakh agency build — at a fraction of the price. Open my demos above and judge for yourself.

Why pay only after preview?

Trust is earned, not promised. I'd rather show you real work than send a quotation PDF. If my site doesn't beat what you have today, you shouldn't pay. That's the whole deal.

Do I need to know technical stuff?

Zero. We chat on WhatsApp in plain Hindi or English. You send me your business info, photos, logo (or I help you make one). I handle everything else.

How long does it take?

3–10 days depending on the plan. Starter: 3–5 days. Growth: 5–7 days. Professional: 7–10 days.

What about hosting and maintenance?

Hosting is free on Vercel (included). If you want me to handle updates and edits after launch, I offer maintenance for ₹999/month. Optional.

Can I get a domain like mybusiness.com or .in?

Yes — included in Growth and Professional plans. Costs around ₹800–₹1,500/year directly to the registrar (you own it).

What if I want to add more pages later?

Easy. Adding pages later is ₹2,500 per page for static, ₹5,000 per page for dynamic. No lock-in.

Fair question. Don't trust the age — judge the work. Open the four live demos above. They're real, deployed, and live. If they look like ₹1 lakh agency work, that's because that's the standard I build to.

Do you do logos / branding too?

Basic logos: yes, included in Growth and Professional. Full brand identity: not my main offer, but I can connect you with someone good.

Yes — proper invoices on every project.
