Questions You Might Have

Why do you work for free until I approve?
Because trust is earned, not claimed. I'd rather show you real work than promise it. If my site doesn't beat what you have, you shouldn't pay me. Simple as that.
How is it that cheap? What's the catch?
No catch. I'm based in India, so my costs are lower than a US agency's. That means I can charge $800 where they charge $3000, and still make a living. The quality is the same — you can see for yourself in my demos.
What if I need more than 3 pages?
No problem. My starting price of $800 covers up to 3 pages. For bigger projects — more pages, online ordering, booking systems, or multi-language sites — I scope it over WhatsApp and quote you a flat price. Always less than what a US agency would charge.
Do you speak English well?
Yes. I communicate primarily through WhatsApp messages, so everything is clear and in writing. For calls, I'm conversational — but most of my clients prefer WhatsApp anyway.
How long does it take?
5 to 7 days from the moment we finish our first chat. I send you a preview on day 3 or 4 so you can give feedback before I finish.
What do you need from me to get started?
Almost nothing. A 15-minute WhatsApp chat about your business. Any photos you want included. Your logo if you have one (I can help make one if not). That's it.
What happens after you deliver?
You own everything. I hand over all files and login details. If you want me to host and maintain it, I offer that for a small monthly fee. If not, no problem.
Why only Indian-owned businesses?
Because I understand the culture, the values, and what your customers expect. I can talk to you like family. And I can design for audiences that most American agencies miss.
